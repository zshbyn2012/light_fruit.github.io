General Facts

  • First Added: January 16, 2019
  • Type: Elemental
  • Rarity: Rare
  • Price: 650,000 Beli or 1,100 Robux
  • Awakening Cost: 14,500 Fragments
  • Best For: Grinding, fast travel, and PvP

Abilities (Unawakened)

  • Light Ray (Z): Fires a high-speed beam of light.
  • Barrage of Light (X): Shoots multiple small light beams.
  • Reflex Kick (C): Teleports and unleashes an explosion.
  • Heavenly Ray Barrage (V): Summons lightning bolts.
  • Gleaming Flight (F): Transforms into light for ultra-fast travel.

Awakened Abilities

  • Divine Arrow (Z): Shoots powerful light arrows.
  • Shining Barrage (X): Fires multiple light projectiles.
  • Reflection Kick (C): Teleports and delivers a strong kick.
  • Sky Beam Barrage (V): Calls down beams of light from above.
  • Lightspeed Flight (F): One of the fastest travel abilities in the game.

Why Light Fruit is Popular

  • Fastest Flight Ability: Makes traveling across seas incredibly quick.
  • Great for Grinding: AoE attacks help defeat multiple enemies at once.
  • Elemental Reflex: Grants immunity to non-Haki attacks from NPCs.
  • Low Mastery Requirements: Easy to unlock moves early in the game.
  • PvP Viability: Awakened Light Fruit has strong ranged attacks.

Trivia

  • Light Fruit was one of the first fruits added to Blox Fruits.
  • It is one of the few fruits with a weapon-based M1 attack (Light Sword).
  • The awakened version changes the M1 attack from a spear to a sword.
  • Light Flight is one of the fastest movement abilities in the game.
  • The title "The First Light" is granted upon awakening one move.
